MSU Student Presents Research at Student Research Symposium 2026

---------------------
I'm proud to share that my students Victor Owino and Asongwe Mutah successfully presented our research at the MSU Student Research Symposium 2026.
Our two posters, titled "Pragmatic Inference Chain (PIC) withLLMs for Adverse Drug Event Detection from Clinical Notes" and "Predicting Adverse Transfusion Reactions using Deep Learning Models" were accepted for the symposium's competitive Poster session.
